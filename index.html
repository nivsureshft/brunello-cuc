Brunello Cucinelli
The suit reinterpreted with Italian "gusto"
Sartorial expertise reinterpreted with modern combinations and character are the aspirations of the modern man.
Men’s tailoring is an art form that has its roots in Italy, in great part. Today, this priceless heritage comes into contact with the needs and impulses of the present-day society.
— Brunello Cucinelli, Founder
In the quote above you find the beauty and uniqueness of men’s tailoring, a  craft that is progressively able to remain a symbol true to itself despite the  everchanging lifestyles. Today, it needs to guide thet 21st century man through the new paradigms of contemporary elegance. One underlining truth remains constant in time: the one garment a man should own is a suit.

In Solomeo – the Italian fashion house’s headquarter – the mission is to create the inimitable ageless look for every man’s needs. They look at traditions and heritage to interpret modern principles of exclusivity and sophistication. For the brand, the suit is the ultimate symbol of effortless elegance and personal style.

Men’s tailoring is an art form that has its roots in Italy, in great part. Today, this priceless heritage comes into contact with the needs and impulses of the present-day society.
— Brunello Cucinelli, Founder
Renewed style guides the new paradigms of contemporary elegance, looking to tradition to decipher the current canons of exclusivity and sophistication, aiming to interpret the codes of our time. Sartoria Solomeo offers a unique experience, a time for dialogue and collaboration.
